Device            tps     kB_read/s    kB_wrtn/s    await    %util
nvme0n1         315.74    3248.80      2156.30    138.70    99.10

Analysis

The system is experiencing a **storage I/O bottleneck**. The very high `%iowait`, elevated `await` time, and disk utilization above 99% indicate that the CPU is spending most of its time waiting for storage operations to complete rather than executing application code. This behavior is consistent with a storage-bound workload rather than a processor limitation.

Conclusion

Capture 1 represents a CPU-bound workload, while Capture 2 demonstrates a storage-bound workload. Comparing both snapshots illustrates how CPU utilization and I/O wait values help administrators distinguish between processing limitations and disk performance problems during Linux performance analysis.
